Miriam Opc is a Spanish illustrator with a meticulous and perfectionist approach, deeply passionate about ink, fantasy, and the mountains —where she often finds creative inspiration.

She works fluently in both traditional and digital media and has collaborated with a variety of clients in publishing and the entertainment industry, including roles as a Concept Artist, Cards Illustrator and 2D Artist. Alongside her illustration work, she also teaches anatomical drawing and mentors aspiring artists, blending her passion for education with her artistic pursuits.

She has a strong preference for traditional tools—particularly ink, markers, and watercolor—which play a central role in her creative process.
